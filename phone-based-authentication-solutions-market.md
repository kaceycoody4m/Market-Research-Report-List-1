<p><ul><li>Single Factor Certification</li><li>Multifactor Certification</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phone-based authentication solutions can be categorized into two market types: Single Factor Certification and Multifactor Certification. Single Factor Certification typically relies on a single method, such as a one-time password (OTP) sent via SMS, to verify user identity. In contrast, Multifactor Certification incorporates multiple authentication methods, such as combining an OTP with biometric data or secondary passwords, enhancing security by requiring more than one form of verification. This layered approach mitigates risks associated with unauthorized access.</p></p>
