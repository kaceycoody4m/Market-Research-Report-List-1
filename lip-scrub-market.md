<p><p>The lip scrub market features two primary types: matte and gelatinous formulations. Matte lip scrubs typically have a dry, powdery texture, often incorporating natural exfoliants like sugar or salt, providing a more robust scrub experience. In contrast, gelatinous lip scrubs have a smoother, more viscous texture that often includes moisturizing agents, creating a gentler exfoliation. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with matte scrubs appealing to those who prefer a deeper cleanse, while gelatinous scrubs are ideal for sensitive lips seeking hydration.</p></p>
