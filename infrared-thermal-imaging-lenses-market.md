<p><p>The infrared thermal imaging lenses market is segmented into three main types: spheres, aspheres, and others. Spherical lenses have a uniform curvature, providing consistent optical performance, while aspherical lenses are designed with a more complex shape to minimize optical aberrations, enhancing image quality. The "others" category encompasses specialized or hybrid lens designs that may combine features of both spherical and aspherical lenses, catering to specific applications in industries such as security, medical imaging, and automotive.</p></p>
