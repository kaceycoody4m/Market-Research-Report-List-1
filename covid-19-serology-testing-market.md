<p><strong>The Covid-19 Serology Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Covid-19 serology testing market includes three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick results using a simple device, making them accessible for point-of-care testing. ELISA is a lab-based method that quantitatively measures antibodies, offering higher sensitivity and specificity. Neutralization Assays assess the ability of antibodies to prevent virus infection, playing a crucial role in understanding immunity and vaccine effectiveness. Each method serves distinct diagnostic and research purposes.</p></p>
