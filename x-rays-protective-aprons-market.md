<p><p>The X-Ray protective aprons market is segmented into three main types based on coverage: front side protection, rear side protection, and side protection. Front side protection aprons shield the wearer’s vital organs from radiation while facing the X-ray source. Rear side protection aprons safeguard against scatter radiation from behind. Side protection aprons, designed for lateral exposure scenarios, ensure comprehensive coverage of the body's sides, crucial in environments with multi-directional radiation exposure, enhancing overall safety for medical professionals and patients.</p></p>
