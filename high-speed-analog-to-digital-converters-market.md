<p><p>The high-speed analog-to-digital converter (ADC) market is primarily categorized into two segments: high-speed and ultra-high-speed converters. High-speed ADCs typically offer sampling rates ranging from several megasamples per second (MSPS) to hundreds of MSPS, suitable for applications like telecommunications and industrial control. Conversely, ultra-high-speed ADCs achieve sampling rates in the gigasamples per second (GSPS) range, catering to demanding applications such as radar, high-frequency trading, and advanced instrumentation. Both segments focus on maximizing precision and minimizing latency in signal processing.</p></p>
