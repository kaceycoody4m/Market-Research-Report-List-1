<p><p>The contour and surface measuring machine market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact measuring machines. Contact contour and surface measuring machines utilize physical probes to touch the surface of the object, providing precise measurements through direct interaction. In contrast, non-contact contour and surface measuring machines employ optical, laser, or other non-invasive technologies to capture surface profiles and contours without physical contact, enabling faster measurements and reducing the risk of damage to delicate surfaces.</p></p>
