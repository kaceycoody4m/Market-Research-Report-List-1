<p><p>The household massage chair market comprises primarily two types: rail massage chairs and trackless massage chairs. Rail massage chairs utilize a fixed track system to guide the massage mechanism along a predetermined path, ensuring targeted pressure on specific areas of the body. In contrast, trackless massage chairs employ a more flexible design, enabling the massage heads to move freely across the chair's surface for a more customizable and versatile massage experience, catering to individual comfort and preferences.</p></p>
