<p><p>The Sedan and Hatchback Wheel Speed Sensor market can be divided into two main types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect wheel rotation, providing accurate speed readings. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on variations in magnetic fields produced by the wheel hubs to generate electrical signals corresponding to speed. Both types are crucial for vehicle safety, stability control, and anti-lock braking systems, contributing to enhanced vehicle performance and reliability.</p></p>
