<p><p>The automotive engine emission control system market encompasses various components essential for reducing harmful emissions. Oxygen sensors monitor exhaust gases to optimize combustion efficiency. EGR valves recirculate a portion of exhaust back to the intake, lowering nitrogen oxide emissions. Catalytic converters transform harmful pollutants into less harmful substances. Air pumps inject fresh air into the exhaust to facilitate combustion. PCV valves manage crankcase gases, while charcoal canisters capture fuel vapors, preventing them from escaping into the atmosphere.</p></p>
