As cancer treatments evolve, TIL therapy is gaining recognition for its ability to achieve significant remissions in patients, particularly those with melanoma and other solid tumors.</p><p>The TIL Therapy Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.3% during the forecast period. Emerging trends include the integration of TIL therapy with other treatment modalities, such as checkpoint inhibitors and targeted therapies, to enhance efficacy and patient outcomes.
