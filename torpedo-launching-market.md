In contrast, deck-mounted units are installed on surface vessels, allowing for quick and efficient firing of torpedoes while on the water's surface. Each system has unique advantages, catering to specific naval strategies and operational requirements, ensuring that various maritime forces can effectively deploy their torpedo capabilities.</p></p>
