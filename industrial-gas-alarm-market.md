<p><p>The Industrial Gas Alarm market features various detection technologies, notably catalytic and infrared optical types. Catalytic gas alarms detect flammable gases through a chemical reaction, providing quick and reliable monitoring in industrial settings. Infrared optical gas alarms, on the other hand, utilize infrared light absorption to identify specific gases, offering high sensitivity and selectivity, particularly for hydrocarbons. Both types are essential for ensuring workplace safety, enabling prompt responses to hazardous gas leaks, and maintaining compliance with safety regulations.</p></p>
